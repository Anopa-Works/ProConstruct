Our Services

End-to-End Project Management & Owner Representation

We structure, manage, and control complex capital projects to ensure predictable delivery — on time, on budget, and to specification.

What We Deliver

1. Owner’s Representative

We act as your independent authority on everything happening on your project. No bias. No contractor influence. Only your interests.

2. Project Planning & Feasibility

Risk assessments, cost modelling, project chartering, and technical validation to ensure the project is viable before money is spent.

3. Contractor Sourcing & Vetting

We identify qualified contractors, evaluate quotations, analyse claims, and compare capabilities based on proven performance.

4. Procurement & Forex Advisory

Transparent procurement management, currency-risk planning, import coordination, and supply-chain protection.

5. Project Supervision & Coordination

Daily oversight, progress monitoring, contractor coordination, and quality control audits across all workstreams.

6. Cost, Time & Scope Control

Baseline schedules, cash-flow tracking, change-order management, and variance reporting for absolute project discipline.

7. Regulatory & Approvals Management

ZIMRA, councils, parastatals, EMA, utility companies — we handle the paperwork and compliance so you avoid delays.

8. Handover & Close-Out

Snag lists, commissioning, documentation, warranties, and final close-out reporting with full transparency.
